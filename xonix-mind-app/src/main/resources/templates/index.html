Welcome to the Xonix!

What is it?

First, our wiki: Xonix-wiki

The general rules are quite simple. At the beginning of the game the whole field is not painted Your bot should paint over as much of the field as possible. Moving across the field the bot leaves a trace behind itself as an infinite snake. As soon as the trace closes (the beginning and the end on the boundary of the field) - the piece inside is considered Collision with other bots resets the unpainted part and sends the bots to their original position. The winner is the one who painted more of the cells.

How to start?

For participation in the game you need to create a bot.

Examples:

  • Java-bot
  • Scala-bot
  • Kotlin-bot

So, as you can see, you can use any jvm languages, but you also need to use Maven for assembling your project.

Important:

  • You need to add in dependencies xonix-mind-commons. Necessary repository is specified in pom.xml in examples.
  • Your bot must to implements interface com.lineate.xonix.mind.model.Bot .
  • Each other bot must be in a separated repository.
